Services

FutureSmart AI provides custom Natural Language Processing (NLP) solutions for companies looking to get ahead of the future. Our dedicated team of Data Scientists and ML Engineers provides an end-to-end solution from data labeling to modeling and deploying an ML model tailored to your specific use case.

Text Classification

Our document classification service can help you automatically sort and categorize documents according to your specific needs. This can save you a considerable amount of time and effort and ensure that your documents are properly handled.

Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis

FutureSmart AI can help you analyze the sentiment of texts concerning particular aspects. This is useful for understanding how customers feel about your product or service.

Named Entity Recognition

Our named entity recognition services can help you automatically extract named entities from text, such as people, locations, organizations, and product names. We can develop custom models to recognize entities specific to your domain or industry.

Chatbot Development

We develop chatbots for businesses of all sizes. Our chatbots are powered by Amazon Lex and AWS Lambda and can be used to automate customer service, sales, and marketing tasks.

GPT-3 Development

We provide a full range of services to help you develop and deploy applications using GPT-3. We can help you with prompt engineering, finetuning your models, and deploying your applications as APIs.

No-Code NLP

We have built multiple GPT-3 powered NLP applications using no-code tools like Bubble, Airtable and Clay.
